Who am I?

My name is Isadora, but you can call me Isa. I am from Brazil and currently study Software Development at Bellevue College. I graduated as a Linguist but have worked in many different areas throughout my life. I moved to US four years ago and decided to become a programmer. In this new career I want to be part of impactful projects, specially for the underprivileged kids from my country.

When I'm not coding I enjoy reading about psycology, business and nutrition. I also love to write and read poems. One of my biggest life goals is to be able to work from anywhere so I can travel and explore the world.
